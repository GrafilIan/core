Sorsogon State University-Bulan Campus Internship Monitoring System

Technological advancements have significantly transformed societal functions, with traditional data monitoring tools in educational institutions such as their manual recordkeeping and its semi-computerized system, demanding a progressive approach that prioritizes transparency and efficiency. Recognizing the importance of accurate data management, a proposal for a Web-based Internship Records Monitoring System has emerged.

This proposed system aims to centralize monitoring, streamline record-keeping, and enhance interaction between students and office management as it caters to both students and administrators, offering distinct features for each to optimize internship management. The primary goal is to establish a secure and effective platform, replacing outdated methods with innovative approaches to ensure accurate and efficient internship management.
